About Us & Our Mission

We are a passionate community from Chembur, Mumbai dedicated to making the city greener and healthier through the simple yet powerful act of planting mango seeds. Our vision is a sustainable Mumbai where every mango seed given today grows into a tree that nurtures future generations.

This initiative is driven by volunteers, supported by donors, and inspired by local families who believe in the power of nature and community.
